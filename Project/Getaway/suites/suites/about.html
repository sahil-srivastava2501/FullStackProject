About Us

Getaway is a specialist tour operator. We create private tailor-made journeys for adventurers and families that bring travel and places alive.
The art of mixology: We design itineraries that carefully blend relaxation with a genuine sense of independence, exploration & adventure.
The local touch: We are proud of our unique network of fantastic local guides who bring their destinations to life like no other.
The carefully chosen accommodation: We choose properties which reflect their location with lovingly designed spaces and warm welcoming hosts.
